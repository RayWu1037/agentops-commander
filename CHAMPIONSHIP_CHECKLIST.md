## Judging Criteria Strategy

### Technical implementation

- Show a complete loop: goal, plan, tool calls, evidence, approval, execution, trace, eval, self-review.
- Keep the code-owned runtime visible so the project is not just a visual mockup.
- Use the tests as proof that the demo has production discipline.

### Design

- Keep the first screen operational and scannable.
- Show the approval gate clearly during the video.
- Use Trace and Evals tabs as the visual proof of observability.

### Potential impact

- Frame the product around lower MTTR, safer production operations, and auditable agentic action.
- Emphasize that the agent assists operators rather than replacing them.

### Creativity

- Lead with the self-improvement loop: Phoenix-style traces and evals inform the next run.
- Make the differentiator clear: not a chatbot, but an observable, evaluated, human-supervised agent.
